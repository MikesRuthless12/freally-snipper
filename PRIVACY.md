## The short version
Freally Snipper is **local-first and privacy-respecting**: **no accounts, no
telemetry, no analytics**. Your captures, recordings, and edits **never leave your
computer** unless *you* save, share, or export them.

## What we collect
**Nothing.** The Licensor does not collect, receive, store, or transmit:

- your screenshots, screen recordings, audio, webcam frames, or edited files;
- your settings, file paths, or usage data;
- any personal information or identifiers.

All of your content and settings stay on your device, in the folders and
configuration locations you control.

## Network use
Freally Snipper works **fully offline** for capture, image editing, screen
recording, and `.fvid` playback. It uses the network **only** when **you** trigger
an optional feature whose component isn't installed yet — specifically the
**on-demand download** of:

- optional machine-learning models (e.g. OCR, translation), and
- optional tools such as **ffmpeg** (used for MP4/WebM video export),

fetched from their respective third-party distributors. These downloads transfer
the component **to** your machine; they are initiated by your action, and **no
personal data or User Content is sent** as part of them beyond the standard
network request needed to fetch the file. Those third-party distributors have
their own privacy practices.

## Microphone, system audio, and webcam
When you enable audio or webcam capture for a recording, that data is used **only**
to produce your recording on your device. It is **never** transmitted to the
Licensor or anyone else, and you control whether each source is captured.

## Children
Freally Snipper is a general-purpose tool, is not directed at children, and
collects no information from anyone.

## Changes
Any change to this policy will be reflected in this document, both in the
application's About panel and in the project repository.
